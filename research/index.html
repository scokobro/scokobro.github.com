Research Activities

I wrote my PhD thesis about how certain ‘semiotic resources’, available to television news camera operators and - theoretically at least - used to express power relations within a society, were mobilised in television news images in Japan.

Having spent a few years in the television news industry before entering academia my publication list is not so long; this is what there is so far…
